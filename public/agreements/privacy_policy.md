
# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: Oct 16, 2025_

**Appsbooth Limited** is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information while using the **Busy.me** application, website, and services ("Service").

By using the Service, you agree to this policy. If you do not, please discontinue use.

---

## Information We Collect

### With Your Permission (via Google API Services):
- **Gmail**: Email headers, content, attachments (when required), labels, folders, metadata.
- **Google Contacts**: Names, email addresses, phones, addresses, job info, notes, groups, profile images.
- **Google Drive**: Files, folders, images, videos, types, metadata, permissions.

### Automatically Collected:
- **Account Info**: Name, email, and profile picture from Google.
- **Usage Info**: Features used, actions taken, session duration.
- **Device Info**: IP address, browser, OS, device ID, connection speed.
- **Cookies**: Used for personalization. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser. Full Cookie Policy is available upon first use.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- Operate, maintain, and personalize the Service
- Authenticate users and enable access
- Respond to inquiries and provide support
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Fulfill legal obligations

**Limited Use of Google Data**:  
Our application integrates with Google APIs to provide essential functionality. We request the following scopes:

- `https://mail.google.com/` – To access Gmail messages, metadata, attachments, and labels when explicitly required by the user within the Busy.me service.
- `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file` – To upload, view, and manage files created or opened by you within Busy.me.
- `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email` – To retrieve your primary Google Account email for authentication and account linking.
- `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.profile` – To personalize your profile with your Google name and avatar.
- `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/contacts.readonly` – To let you view and select contacts when sharing items. We do **not** store or modify your contacts.

We use this data **only** to deliver in-app features and **never** for advertising or unauthorized sharing.

> Our use and transfer of information through Google APIs complies with the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including Limited Use requirements.

Human access to your data occurs only with your consent or when required for debugging, security, or legal reasons.
